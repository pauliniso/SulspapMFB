POS Payment Solutions

Experience seamless banking with the Sulspap Mobile App. From checking balances to securing funding, manage your banking needs effortlessly.

Our Point-of-Sale (POS) service allows you to carry out electronic transactions using a POS terminal. The device enables customers to make payments using debit cards and also supports basic banking services such as cash withdrawal, transfers, and bill payments.

Our POS Services are designed to help businesses, agents, and merchants offer fast, secure, and convenient payment solutions to their customers. Whether you operate a retail shop, market stall, supermarket, pharmacy, or agency banking outlet, our POS service helps you serve customers better while growing your income.

Why Choose Sulspap POS Services?

  • Reliable and easy-to-use POS terminals
  • Competitive transaction charges
  • Prompt settlement of funds
  • Dedicated customer support
  • Trusted and regulated banking partner

Services You Can Offer with Our POS Terminal?

With a Sulspap POS terminal, you can:

  • Accept card payments for goods and services
  • Process cash withdrawals for customers
  • Transfer funds to bank accounts
  • Pay utility bills and other services
  • Offer cash-in and cash-out services
  • Serve customers quickly without handling large cash volumes
Steps to Onboard As a POS Agent

Getting started as a POS agent with Sulspap Microfinance Bank is simple and straightforward. Follow the steps below to begin offering POS services and earning income.

    Step 1: Visit Any Sulspap Branch

    Visit the nearest Sulspap Microfinance Bank branch to express your interest in becoming a POS agent.

    Step 2: Complete the Application Form

    Fill out the POS agent application form and provide the required information accurately.

    Step 3: Submit Required Documents

    Provide the necessary documents for verification, which may include:

    • Valid means of identification
    • Proof of business location or residence
    • Passport photograph
    • Any other document required by the bank

    Step 4: Account Opening or Verification

    You must have an active account with Sulspap Microfinance Bank.
    If you do not have one, our staff will assist you in opening an account.

    Step 5: POS Terminal Setup

    Upon approval, you will be issued a POS terminal and guided on how to use it effectively.

    Step 6: Training and Activation

    You will receive basic training on POS operations, transaction procedures, and security best practices before your terminal is activated.

    Step 7: Start Transacting

    Once activated, you can begin offering POS services to customers and earn commissions on every transaction.

Become a POS agent with Sulspap Microfinance Bank today and grow your income while serving your community..

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
  • Who can become a POS agent?

    • Traders, shop owners, entrepreneurs, and individuals with a stable business location can apply to become POS agents with Sulspap Microfinance Bank.

  • How much does the POS terminal cost?

    • The cost and applicable charges will be communicated during the application process, as they may vary based on the POS package and services offered.

  • How do I earn as a POS agent?

    • POS agents earn commissions on transactions carried out on the terminal, including withdrawals, transfers, and bill payments.

  • What happens if my POS terminal has issues?

    • Our support team is available to assist you with technical issues, terminal maintenance, or transaction concerns.

  • Can I operate the POS service anywhere?

    • You are expected to operate from an approved business location to ensure security, reliability, and compliance with banking regulations.

  • What services can I offer with a Sulspap POS terminal?
    • Card payments for goods and services
    • Cash withdrawals
    • Fund transfers
    • Bill payments
    • Cash-in and cash-out services
