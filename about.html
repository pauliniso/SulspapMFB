Who We Are!

Sulspap Microfinance Bank Limited is a leading lending and financial services institution, licensed by the Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN) in 2015 to operate as a Microfinance Bank. The Bank was established to harness emerging financial opportunities by providing accessible banking services to the underserved yet economically active population that does not meet the requirements of traditional commercial banks.

With a decade-long track record of delivering reliable, ethical, and customer-focused credit solutions, Sulspap Microfinance Bank has continued to demonstrate sustained growth and industry impact. In August 2025, the Bank proudly marked its 10th anniversary, celebrating ten years of operational excellence, resilience, and contribution to financial inclusion..

Since inception, Sulspap Microfinance Bank has been committed to empowering individuals, entrepreneurs, and businesses by providing accessible financing that supports personal advancement, business expansion, and economic development. Our services are designed to meet the evolving financial needs of our customers while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism, transparency, and regulatory compliance.,

Over the past ten years, we have built a strong reputation for trust, efficiency, and responsiveness. Our success is anchored on prudent risk management, innovative lending practices, and a deep understanding of our customers’ financial realities. By combining industry expertise with technology-driven processes, we ensure timely loan disbursement, flexible repayment options, and exceptional customer service.

Our Vision

To be a leading provider of inclusive microfinance services to individuals, low-income groups, and micro enterprises, driven by technology and a highly motivated workforce.

Our Mision

To provide accessible and sustainable financial services through ethical, technology-driven, and customer-focused banking that promotes financial inclusion and economic growth.
