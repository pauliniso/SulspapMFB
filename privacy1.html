INTRODUCTION

Sulspap Microfinance Bank Ltd ("we," "us," or "our") is a financial institution committed to protecting the privacy, confidentiality, and security of personal and financial information entrusted to us by our applicants, borrowers, customers, and website users.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, protect, and disclose personal data, and outlines your rights regarding that information, in compliance with applicable data protection and financial regulations.

Important Notice: We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Material changes will be communicated through our website or other appropriate channels.

SCOPE OF THIS POLICY

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • Loan applicants and borrowers
  • Website and mobile application users
  • Business partners and guarantors
  • All individuals communicating with us through any channel

This policy governs all personal data processed by Sulspap Microfinance Bank, whether collected online or offline.

REGULATORY COMPLIANCE FRAMEWORK

We process personal data in strict compliance with applicable laws and regulations governing data protection and financial services, including but not limited to:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR)
  • Fair Credit Reporting Act (FCRA)
  • Anti-Money Laundering (AML) requirements
  • Know Your Customer (KYC) requirements
  • All applicable national and local data protection laws

We are committed to handling personal information lawfully, securely, and transparently.

TYPES OF DATA WE COLLECT

1. Personal Identification Information

  • Full name
  • Date of birth
  • Government-issued identification (passport, national ID, driver's license, BVN)
  • Tax identification or social security number
  • Marital status and dependents (where applicable)
  • Photograph and biometric data (where required)

2. Contact Information

  • Residential and mailing address
  • Email address
  • Telephone and mobile numbers
  • Emergency contact information

3. Financial Information

  • Bank account details
  • Income and employment information
  • Credit history and credit scores
  • Transaction history
  • Loan applications, amounts, repayment schedules, and collateral details
  • Financial statements and tax records

4. Technical and Usage Data

  • IP address and geolocation data
  • Device identifiers and specifications
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Website usage patterns, cookies, and analytics data
  • Login credentials and authentication records

HOW WE COLLECT YOUR DATA

We collect personal data through the following methods:

  • Loan applications submitted online or offline
  • Direct communications via email, phone, or in-person meetings
  • Credit bureaus and financial institutions
  • Identity verification and authentication providers
  • Website cookies and tracking technologies
  • Public records and databases
  • Legal and regulatory authorities

HOW WE USE YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION

We process your personal information based on your consent, our contractual obligations, legal requirements, or our legitimate business interests for the following purposes:

Service Delivery and Account Management

  • Evaluating and processing loan applications
  • Assessing creditworthiness and eligibility
  • Disbursing funds and managing loan accounts
  • Processing transactions, payments, and repayments
  • Administering your ongoing relationship with us

Compliance and Risk Management

  • Verifying your identity through KYC procedures
  • Conducting AML screening and sanctions checks
  • Managing credit, operational, and reputational risk
  • Detecting, preventing, and investigating fraud and financial crime
  • Meeting regulatory reporting and disclosure obligations
  • Resolving disputes and enforcing our agreements

Customer Service and Communication

  • Providing customer support and responding to inquiries
  • Sending account notifications, transaction updates, and payment reminders
  • Communicating service alerts, policy changes, and important announcements
  • Conducting customer surveys and collecting feedback
  • Sharing information about new products, services, features, or promotional offers

Opt-Out: You may opt out of promotional communications at any time by contacting dpo@Sulspapmfb.com.

Business Improvement and Analytics

  • Analyzing aggregated and anonymized data to enhance service quality
  • Measuring performance and optimizing platform functionality
  • Strengthening our information technology infrastructure and security
  • Utilizing automated decision-making, profiling, and artificial intelligence tools to refine credit assessments, improve customer experience, and develop innovative financial solutions
  • Conducting internal audits and quality assurance

Third-Party Collaboration

Where necessary, we collaborate with Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN) licensed financial institutions and authorized service providers to facilitate account creation, value storage, payment processing, or other banking services on your behalf.

Additional Processing

Should we intend to process your personal information for purposes not specified in this Privacy Policy, we will provide prior notice and, where required by law, obtain your explicit consent.

LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING

We process your personal data based on one or more of the following legal grounds:

  • Contractual Necessity: To fulfill our obligations under loan agreements and service contracts
  • Legal Compliance: To meet regulatory, tax, and legal obligations
  • Legitimate Interests: To operate our business, manage risk, prevent fraud, and improve our services
  • Consent: Where you have provided explicit consent for specific processing activities

Your Right: You have the right to withdraw consent at any time where processing is based solely on consent.

DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

Data Storage

  • Personal data is stored on secure servers located in Nigeria and other designated jurisdictions
  • We utilize cloud service providers that comply with internationally recognized security standards, including ISO 27001 and SOC 2
  • Data backups are maintained in accordance with industry best practices

Security Measures

We implement comprehensive administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information, including:

  • End-to-end encryption of data at rest and in transit
  • Role-based access controls and principle of least privilege
  • Multi-factor authentication for system access
  • Regular vulnerability assessments, penetration testing, and security audits
  • Secure development practices and code reviews
  • Mandatory employee data protection and security training
  • Incident response and business continuity protocols

Security Advisory: For your protection, Sulspap Microfinance Bank will never request your Personal Identification Number (PIN), Account Identification Number (AIN), Dynamic PIN, or Bank Verification Number (BVN) via email, phone, SMS, or written correspondence. You are solely responsible for safeguarding your authentication credentials.

DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share your information only when necessary and under strict confidentiality obligations with:

Service Providers and Business Partners

  • Credit bureaus and financial institutions
  • Payment processors and transaction facilitators
  • Cloud hosting and IT service providers
  • Identity verification and fraud prevention services
  • Legal, accounting, audit, and consulting firms
  • Debt collection agencies (where applicable)

All third parties are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

Regulatory and Legal Disclosure

We may disclose personal data to regulatory authorities, law enforcement agencies, courts, or tribunals when:

  • Required by law, legal process, or court order
  • Necessary to comply with regulatory obligations
  • Required to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our customers
  • Necessary to detect, prevent, or address fraud or security issues

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, or sale of assets, your personal data may be transferred to the successor entity, subject to the same privacy protections outlined in this policy.

INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

Where personal data is transferred across international borders, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by relevant authorities
  • Adequacy decisions recognizing equivalent data protection standards
  • Binding corporate rules and contractual protections
  • Technical and organizational security measures

We ensure that all international transfers comply with applicable data protection regulations.

DATA RETENTION

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill the purposes for which it was collected
  • Comply with contractual obligations
  • Meet legal, regulatory, tax, and accounting requirements
  • Resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and protect our legal rights

Retention periods are determined based on:

  • The nature and sensitivity of the data
  • Applicable legal and regulatory requirements (typically 5-10 years for financial records)
  • The purposes for which the data is processed

Upon expiration of the applicable retention period, personal data is securely deleted or anonymized in accordance with our data retention and disposal policies.

YOUR DATA PROTECTION RIGHTS

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right of Access

Request copies of your personal data

Right to Rectification

Correct inaccurate or incomplete information

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)

Right to Restriction

Limit how we process your data in certain circumstances

Right to Object

Object to processing based on legitimate interests

Right to Data Portability

Receive your data in a structured, commonly used format

Right to Withdraw Consent

Withdraw previously given consent at any time

Right to Lodge a Complaint

File a complaint with the relevant data protection authority

Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer using the contact details provided below. We will respond to your request within the timeframe required by applicable law (typically 30 days).

Please note that certain rights may be subject to limitations based on legal obligations or legitimate business interests.

COOKIES AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to:

  • Enable essential website functionality
  • Enhance user experience and personalization
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Improve security and detect fraudulent activity
  • Deliver relevant content and advertisements

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Required for website operation
  • Performance Cookies: Collect anonymous usage statistics
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences
  • Targeting Cookies: Deliver personalized content

Managing Cookies

You may manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may limit website functionality. For more information, please refer to our Cookie Policy.

DATA BREACH NOTIFICATION

In the event of a personal data breach that poses a risk to your rights and freedoms, we will:

  • Promptly investigate and contain the incident
  • Assess the nature and scope of the breach
  • Notify affected individuals within 72 hours where required by law
  • Report the breach to relevant supervisory authorities as legally mandated
  • Implement remedial measures to prevent recurrence
  • Provide guidance on steps you can take to protect yourself

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Important: Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect, process, or retain personal data from minors. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, we will take prompt steps to delete such data.

Parents or guardians who believe we may have collected information from a minor should contact us immediately.

THIRD-PARTY LINKS

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, applications, or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you access.

CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

When we make material changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this policy
  • Notify you through our website, email, or other appropriate channels
  • Obtain your consent where required by law

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
