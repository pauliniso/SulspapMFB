Our Group Loan

Our Group Loan is a structured credit facility for a small, organized group of individuals—typically traders, artisans, farmers, or micro-entrepreneurs, who voluntarily come together to access financing. Members of the group usually share similar economic activities, operate within the same community, and have an existing level of trust among themselves.

Rather than requiring traditional collateral such as land, vehicles, or fixed assets, the loan relies on a group guarantee system. Each member acts as a guarantor for the others, meaning the group collectively takes responsibility for ensuring that all members repay their individual loan obligations. If any member is unable to repay as scheduled, the group is expected to support that member to prevent default.

Group Loans are typically approved based on the group’s overall credibility, repayment capacity, savings behavior, and internal discipline. While loan amounts are issued to individuals, the approval, monitoring, and repayment are managed within the group framework. Repayments are often made at regular group meetings, which take place on a weekly bases.

Key Benefits of Our Group Loan

  • No Collateral Needed: You can access loans through group guarantees, even without assets.
  • Easier Loan Access: Group support helps more people qualify for credit.
  • On-Time Repayment Support: Group monitoring encourages everyone to repay promptly.
  • Affordable Loan Terms: Shared responsibility allows for competitive interest rates.
  • Savings Discipline: Regular savings are encouraged to support financial stability.
  • Strong Peer Support: Group members support and motivate one another.
  • Financial Inclusion: More individuals and small businesses gain access to banking services.

How Group Loans Work

  1. Group Formation: Customers form a group (usually 5–30 people) with members who know and trust each other.
  2. Registration & Training: The bank registers the group and provides basic training on loan use, savings, and repayment rules.
  3. Loan Application: Each member applies for a loan amount based on their business capacity.
  4. Approval & Disbursement: Loans are approved individually but disbursed under the group structure.
  5. Repayment: Members repay weekly or monthly. If one member defaults, the group supports repayment.
Application Requirements
  • Customer opens a Savings account with Sulspap MFB
  • Duly completed application form
  • A valid means of ID (national ID, driver's license, voter's card or international passport)
  • 2 guarantors
  • Current utility bill
  • 4 recent passport-size photographs
