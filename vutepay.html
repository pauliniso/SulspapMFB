VutePay – Virtual Account Solution

VutePay is Sulspap Microfinance Bank’s secure and innovative Virtual Account solution, designed to make receiving and managing payments simple, fast, and stress-free. With VutePay, you get a unique account number that allows you to receive payments just like a regular bank account—without opening multiple accounts. Whether you are an individual, a trader, or a business owner, VutePay helps you collect payments conveniently while keeping your finances organized.

Key Benefits of VutePay
  • Supports transfers from any bank.
  • Payments are credited instantly or within minutes.
  • It helps you monitor all incoming payments clearly and accurately.
  • Real-time transaction alerts
  • Enjoy the comfort of receiving payments anytime, anywhere without delay.
  • All VutePay transactions are protected and monitored in line with banking security standards.

How VutePay Works

VutePay is a Virtual Account linked directly to your Sulspap Microfinance Bank account. It comes with a unique account number that you can share with customers, partners, or payers to receive transfers easily from any bank. Once a payment is made, the funds are automatically credited to your main account, giving you instant access and clear transaction records.

Who Can Use VutePay?

Our VutePay service is ideal for:

  • Businesses receiving frequent customer payments
  • Traders and merchants collecting daily sales
  • Organizations collecting dues, fees, or contributions
  • Individuals who want a simple and secure way to receive payments

How VutePay Works

  1. You request a Virtual Account from Sulspap Microfinance Bank
  2. We assign you a unique Virtual Account number
  3. You share the account number with customers or payers
  4. Payments are made via bank transfer
  5. Funds are automatically credited to your main account

No manual tracking. No delays, Simple. Secure. Reliable.
